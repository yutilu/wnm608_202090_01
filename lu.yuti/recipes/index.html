Sushi Rolls
  • 2 cups sushi rice, rinsed and drained
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Bamboo sushi mat
  • 8 nori sheets
  • Some vegetables (Any vegetables you like)
  • 3 ounces imitation crab meat or salmon (Any meat you like) and cut
  • 1 medium ripe avocado, peeled and cut
  • Reduced-sodium soy sauce, prepared wasabi, and pickled ginger slices
Instructions
  1. In a large saucepan, combine rice and water; let stand for 30 minutes. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until water is absorbed and rice is tender. Remove from the heat. Let stand, covered, for 10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, combine the vinegar, sugar, and salt, stirring until sugar is dissolved.
  3. 3Transfer rice to a large shallow bowl; drizzle with vinegar mixture. With a wooden paddle or spoon, stir the rice with a slicing motion to cool slightly. Cover with a damp cloth to keep moist. (Rice mixture may be made up to 2 hours ahead and stored at room temperature, covered with a damp towel. Do not refrigerate.)
  4. Place the sushi mat on a work surface so mat rolls away from you; line with plastic wrap. Place 3/4 cup rice on plastic. With moistened fingers, press rice into an 8-in. square. Top with one nori sheet.
  5. Arrange a some of vegetables, crab or salmon, and avocado about 1-1/2 in. from the bottom edge of the nori sheet. Roll up rice mixture over filling, using the bamboo mat to lift and compress the mixture while rolling; remove plastic wrap as you roll.
  6. Remove mat; take out the sushi. Cover with plastic wrap. Repeat with the remaining ingredients to make eight rolls. Cut each into eight pieces. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and ginger slices if desired.
Wonton Soup
  • Prepare the wonton you like (pork, shrimp, vegetables)
  • 1 tablespoon light soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 tablespoon chicken essence
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt
  • Some vegetables (Any vegetables you like), optional
  • 3 cups chicken stock
Instructions
  1. Bring the chicken stock to a rolling boil.
  2. Drop wontons in, and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Drop vegetables in, and cook for 2 minuets.
  4. Pour the soup, wonton, and vegetables into a bow;. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, chicken essence, and salt.
  5. Garnish with chopped green onion. (optional)
Jam Bread
  • Prepare bread, 2 slices (whole wheat bread, whole grain bread, any))
  • Any jam (Strawberry jam, cherry jam, peach jam)
  • Peanut butter
  • Any fruit, and cut (Strawberry, blueberry, raspberry)
  • One table-knife
Instructions
  1. Take out a piece of bread and spread it on the plate.
  2. Take out the jam and peanut butter with a knife.
  3. Spread the cut fruit over the jam.
  4. Take a piece of bread and cover it on the fruit, and ready to sevier.
